How to use

Controls

Screen burn-in protection

To protect OLED and AMOLED displays, the clock subtly shifts its position every 10 minutes in a slow circular pattern. Dark Mode further reduces the risk of burn-in. Protection does not apply in embedded mode. Use ?burnin=false to disable.

Install as app (PWA)

This site works as a Progressive Web App. You can install it on your phone or computer for a full-screen clock and offline use. Use your browser’s menu (e.g. “Add to Home Screen” or “Install app”) to install.

Timezones

Add ?tz= to the URL with any IANA timezone:

www.clocksimulator.com/?tz=America/New_York
www.clocksimulator.com/?tz=Europe/London
www.clocksimulator.com/?tz=Asia/Tokyo

Multi-clock dashboard

Show multiple clocks at once by separating timezones with commas. This automatically enables dashboardMode. Use &rows= to control the grid layout:

www.clocksimulator.com/?tz=UTC,Europe/Helsinki,America/New_York
www.clocksimulator.com/?tz=UTC,Europe/Helsinki,America/New_York,Asia/Tokyo&rows=2 (dashboardMode)

Embedding

Embed the clock on any website via an <iframe>. Use the built-in embed panel (ⓘ → Embed this clock) to configure and copy the code.
